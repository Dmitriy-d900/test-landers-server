Why Choose Oil & Gas?

The oil and gas sector remains a cornerstone of India’s energy landscape, driving large-scale projects from offshore exploration to pipeline development. As global demand evolves, professionals with specialized skills in reservoir management and drilling technologies are positioned for varied roles across multinational operators, service contractors and regulatory agencies.

Curriculum Highlights

Our curriculum is curated in partnership with seasoned industry practitioners at the Indian Institute of Science, covering seismic interpretation, production optimization, health and safety regulations, and emerging trends such as digital oilfield technologies.

Career Outcomes

Graduates have progressed into roles such as drilling engineer, reservoir analyst, HSE coordinator and project manager. Alumni reports indicate successful placements with major energy firms, EPC contractors and consulting organizations across India.

Course Structure

Each plan is divided into modules that build core competencies and practical insights. Delivery combines self-paced resources, live online sessions and on-campus workshops.

Hands-on assignments and assessments ensure application of theory to real-world scenarios, reinforcing critical thinking and technical problem solving.

Did You Know? India’s first oil well was drilled in 1889 at Digboi, Assam, and the country now ranks among the top ten global producers. Continuous innovation has shaped downstream refineries and upstream exploration methods over more than a century.
